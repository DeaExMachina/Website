Spaghetti Putanesca

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti noodles
  • Boiling water
  • Tomatoes
  • Olives
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Secret ingredient: Magic!

Instructions

  1. Throw your Spaghetti into the boiling water and don't care about their cries for help.
  2. After they're finished, mix them with the mashed tomatoes and the olives.
  3. Apply Parmesan and magic before serving your Spaghetti.

Dreamed Spaghetti

I invented this recipe in my dream. Tastes really good!

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti noodles
  • Boiling water
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Prosciutto ham
  • Oil
  • Spices, e.g. garlic, parsley, chili ...

Instructions

  1. Throw your Spaghetti into the boiling water and don't care about their cries for help.
  2. After they're finished, mix them with oil and spices.
  3. Add Parmesan and Prosciutto before serving your spaghetti with a smile.
