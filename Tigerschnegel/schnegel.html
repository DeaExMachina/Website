Leopard Slugs

Some interesting facts:

  • Leopard Slugs are all individually dotted or spotted - their shade of colour is as unique as a fingerprint!
  • They are actually the best friends of each gardener as they eat rotten plant parts and other slugs.
  • Because it's so amazing, I have to repeat it: They eat eggs of other slugs, and sometimes, they even eat grown ones as well!
  • They have the most amazing way to reproduce - they hang down on a shared thread of slime, "cuddle" and put their penises together over their heads.
  • Their Latin name is limax maximus.
